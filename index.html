Survey Disclaimer

The purpose of this research project is to determine what effect Virtual Reality technology has on its users.

This is a research project being conducted by April Portman as part of a research project at Falmouth University.

You are invited to participate in this research project because you are the ideal candidate with experience using VR technology.

Your participation in this research study is voluntary.

You may choose not to participate.

If you decide to participate in this research survey, you may withdraw at any time.

If you decide not to participate in this study or if you withdraw from participating at any time, you will not be penalized.

The procedure involves filling an online survey that will take approximately 15 minutes.

Your responses will be confidential and we do not collect identifying information such as your full name, email address or IP address.

The survey questions will be about your general use of VR.

We will do our best to keep your information confidential.

All data is stored in a password protected electronic format.

To help protect your confidentiality, the surveys will not contain information that will personally identify you.

The results of this study will be used for scholarly purposes only and may be shared with Falmouth University representatives.

If you have any questions about the research study, please contact aprilportman26@gmail.com

This research has been reviewed according to Falmouth University procedures for research involving human subjects.

Thank you for taking part in this survey
